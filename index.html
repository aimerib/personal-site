About Me

While I enjoyed that work, I always had a fascination with software development and decided to make the switch in 2019. I am excited to be pursuing my passion and am constantly looking for ways to improve and learn more. One thing that sets me apart is my international background. I was born in the United States, but I spent 20 years growing up in Brazil and am fluent in Portuguese. This unique perspective has influenced my approach to software development and has given me a deeper understanding of different cultures and ways of thinking. In addition to my work as a software engineer, I am also a huge mental health advocate. I believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and I strive to create a positive and supportive work environment for myself and those around me. Outside of work, family life is extremely important to me. I have a young son and am happily married. When I'm not coding, you can find me indulging in my many hobbies. I have more hobbies than I can count, and I love nothing more than picking up new ones. Currently, I've been fascinated with 3D printing, woodworking, electronics projects with the Arduino board, and baking. I'm always looking for new challenges and ways to express my creativity. Finally, I also have a military background, having served in the Navy reserves for 4 years. I believe that my time in the military has taught me valuable skills such as discipline, teamwork, and the importance of attention to detail, all of which have been invaluable in my career as a software engineer.
